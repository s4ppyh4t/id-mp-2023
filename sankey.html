How are people permanently migrating to Australia in the past two decades?

Data period: 2000 - 8/2021

Background

The visualisation below describes flow of migrants' pathways to Australia, starting from their departure from different regions, their application through different visa streams and their final destination in specific States and Territories in Australia as permanent residences (PR).

Hovering through each item/node and their connections highlights necessary relevant visual functionalities, assisting in differentiating, comparing and understanding the flow of migrations from different geographical regions in the world to Australia within the time frame of 2000-2021.

Each item/node element represents the quantity of migrants (number of people) from such regions, visa streams and destination state/territory relative to one another.

Data retrieved from ABS (2021)
